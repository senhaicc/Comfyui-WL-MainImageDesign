[HUMAN ANATOMY INTEGRITY - CRITICAL] ⚠️
=============================================================

When scenes involve people/riders/models, STRICTLY ensure anatomical correctness:
- Hand Integrity: 5 fingers complete, joints naturally bent, no extra or missing fingers
- Arm Integrity: Both arms proportionally correct, elbow joints natural, no breaks or deformations
- Posture Logic: Body movements follow physics, balanced center of gravity, achievable poses
- Wearing Logic: Gear/gloves/helmets worn correctly, straps in proper positions
- Avoid Deformations: NO twisted fingers, unnatural joint angles, merged limbs

Recommended Human Presentation (to reduce errors):
- PREFER: Side view/back view/silhouettes - reduces need for detailed hand rendering
- PREFER: Hands obscured by product (e.g., gloves showing only back of hand on handlebars)
- PREFER: Distant figures (person <30% of frame, less detail required)
- PREFER: Partial close-ups (wrist down only, holding product)
- CAUTION: Full frontal open palms, complex hand gestures, multi-person interactions

If human anatomy cannot be guaranteed correct, prefer:
- Product solo display + environmental hints (blurred motorcycle background)
- Abstract human silhouettes
- Only show body parts covered by product (gloved hands, helmeted head profile)

[SCENE MODE GUIDELINES]

★ 场景融合模式 (Scene Integration Mode) - DEFAULT ★
Products must be shown in real usage contexts with story and emotion:
- Motorcycle helmets: Rider wearing helmet, profile/back view, road/track/city background with speed atmosphere
- Riding gloves: Close-up of hands gripping motorcycle handlebars, showing glove details and dashboard
- Riding suits: Model wearing the suit in confident stance, motorcycle or garage environment background
- Protective gear: Rider wearing gear in dynamic riding scene, emphasizing protection
- Bluetooth headsets: Rider with headset inside helmet, communication/music scene

Product must be the visual focus, but with environmental atmosphere creating aspiration and immersion.
FORBIDDEN: Pure white backgrounds, product-only static shots, ID-photo style compositions.

★ 纯产品棚拍模式 (Studio Product Mode) ★
Professional studio style with designed backgrounds (not plain white):
- Background: Color gradients or subtle textures with light effects
- Product: 45° classic angle, floating display, professional 3-point lighting
- Details: Refined material highlights, reflections, and shadows
- Additions: Subtle halos, particles, geometric lines as design elements
FORBIDDEN: Plain white without design, flat lay top-down, no lighting depth.

★ 骑行动态场景 (Dynamic Riding Scene Mode) ★
Must create impactful dynamic riding visuals:
- Scenes: Highway riding, track cornering, off-road adventure, city commute
- Dynamics: Speed blur, dust flying, hair/fabric flowing
- Atmosphere: Golden hour backlight, neon nights, rain reflections - dramatic lighting
- Product: Naturally integrated as part of rider's gear
Product must be clearly identifiable, but overall image must have cinematic impact.
FORBIDDEN: Static placement, no people, no scene.
